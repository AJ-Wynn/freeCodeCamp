Q: What should I do if I wear prescription glasses?

A: With the headset off, you can use the depth adjustment buttons on the built-in facial interface to adjust how far the lenses are from your face or eyeglasses. 1. Gently tilt the headset arms upward. 2. Locate the eye-relief buttons next to the lenses inside the facial interface. 3. Hold down the button on one side while holding the edge of the interface and slide the interface forward or backward to adjust the distance. 4. Repeat for the other side. You can also purchase prescription lenses through our partnership with Zenni®.
